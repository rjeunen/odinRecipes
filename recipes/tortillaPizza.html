tortilla Pizza

Description

tortilla, round, thin, flat bread of Mexico made from unleavened cornmeal or, less commonly, wheat flour. Traditionally, the corn (maize) for tortillas was boiled with unslaked lime to soften the kernels and loosen the hulls.

ingredients

  • 1 (8 inch) soft flour tortilla
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 3 tablespoons tomato sauce
  • 1 cooked chicken breast, sliced
  • ½ green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 green onions, finely chopped
  • ⅓ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 pinch dried oregano

Steps

  1. Brush the tortilla with olive oil, season, and bake.
  2. Top with tomato sauce and toppings. Sprinkle it with cheese.
  3. Bake again until the cheese is melted, then season it with oregano.
